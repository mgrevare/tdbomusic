About Tdbo

Taran Bowman aka Tdbo is an American rap artist born and raised in Kansas City. After attending the University of Kansas he entered the music industry full time as a producer and ghost writer. Tdbo records out of a self-made studio in his basement in KCMO. All of the studio quality equipment was purchased throughout high school and college with funds from Taran's entrepreneurial ventures. Heavily influenced by Eminem, Tech N9ne, and Logic, Tdbo fills his bars with a combination of fun wordplay and rock hard liftstyle.

Production, Hip-Hop, marketing, we study it all. Hit us up if you want to work together. management@tdbomusic.com
